Why invest in derivatives?

Derivatives are a form of financial contract

Between two or more parties based upon the assets. The most common form of derivatives are futures, forwards, options, swaps.

They can be used to hedge or insure against risk on an asset

It can also be used for speculation in betting on the future price of an assets
